Community development isn't a career stage — it's been a consistent thread across military service, veteran advocacy, neighborhood outreach, and international volunteer work. This page documents that work, past and present.

Active

Mindelo, Cabo Verde

Currently Active — 2024 to Present

Upon arriving in Mindelo, I made a deliberate choice to listen and learn before proposing anything. Through conversations with students, community members, and local organizations, three areas of engagement emerged. All work is done independently and on a volunteer basis.

American Spaces Program

U.S. Embassy — American Spaces at the University of Cabo Verde

Supporting the U.S. Embassy's American Spaces program through weekly English conversation sessions and professional development discussions for university students. Contributing to early-stage programming concepts including skills-based workshops, career development sessions, and community knowledge-sharing events, in coordination with the local program coordinator.

Nonprofit Support

Simabo — Local Nonprofit

Providing English language coaching and communication support to staff at Simabo, a community-serving nonprofit organization based in Mindelo. Supporting their outreach and internal communication capacity.

Community Mapping

Cabo Verde Community Services & Collaboration Directory

Contributing to a community mapping and outreach initiative to better understand local services and needs, connecting organizations, service providers, and community resources through bilingual outreach and needs assessment. The goal is to make it easier for people and organizations in Mindelo to find each other and collaborate.

2012 – 2014

Veteran & Community Outreach

Azusa, CA — 2013
APU Military & Veteran Resource Fair
Azusa Pacific University — First of its kind

Conceived, organized, and executed APU's first-ever Military and Veteran Resource Fair in partnership with 50+ service agencies. Provided employment, education, housing, and counseling services, plus $5,000 in clothing and food donations to the local veteran community.

Azusa, CA — 2013
APU Veteran Awareness Week
Azusa Pacific University — First of its kind

Planned and executed APU's first Veteran Awareness Week — a multi-day campus event covering military-civilian career transition, veteran benefits, and personal finance education. Recognized with three Certificates of Congressional Recognition.

San Gabriel Valley — 2013
Heroes in the Shadows Stand Down
San Gabriel Valley Veterans Employment Committee

Co-organized the 3rd Annual Heroes in the Shadows Homeless Veteran Stand Down, providing housing, medical, and employment assistance to 500+ disadvantaged veterans and their families.

2009 – 2011

Young Adult Community Outreach

San Diego, CA

Revolver Monday Night Outreach Ministry — The Rock Church

Served as Operations Director for The Rock Church's Revolver young-adult outreach ministry in downtown San Diego. Led a team of five in weekly event operations, using art and music to build community and belonging among a diverse urban young-adult population. Facilitated interactions between volunteers, regulars, and newcomers to create an environment of acceptance and connection.
